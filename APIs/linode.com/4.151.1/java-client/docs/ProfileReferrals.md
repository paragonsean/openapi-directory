

# ProfileReferrals

|**completed** | **Integer** | The number of completed signups with your referral code.  |  [optional] [readonly] |
|**credit** | **Integer** | The amount of account credit in US Dollars issued to you through the referral program.  |  [optional] [readonly] |
|**pending** | **Integer** | The number of pending signups with your referral code.  You will not receive credit for these signups until they are completed.  |  [optional] [readonly] |
|**total** | **Integer** | The number of users who have signed up with your referral code.  |  [optional] [readonly] |
|**url** | **String** | Your referral url, used to direct others to sign up for Linode with your referral code.  |  [optional] [readonly] |
