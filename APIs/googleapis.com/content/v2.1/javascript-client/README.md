Authentication schemes defined for the API:
### Oauth2


- **Type**: OAuth
- **Flow**: implicit
- **Authorization URL**: https://accounts.google.com/o/oauth2/auth
- **Scopes**: 
  - https://www.googleapis.com/auth/content: Manage your product listings and accounts for Google Shopping

### Oauth2c


- **Type**: OAuth
- **Flow**: accessCode
- **Authorization URL**: https://accounts.google.com/o/oauth2/auth
- **Scopes**: 
  - https://www.googleapis.com/auth/content: Manage your product listings and accounts for Google Shopping
