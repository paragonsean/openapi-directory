

# GroupRegion

Provides information about an area within a country that a group is associated with (eg. a state in the US or a province in Canada).  May be null.

|------------ | ------------- | ------------- | -------------|
|**abbreviation** | **String** | A 2 letter abbreviation for the region (is not guaranteed to be globally unique but is unique among all the regions in the country). |  [optional] |
|**name** | **String** | The name of the region. |  [optional] |
