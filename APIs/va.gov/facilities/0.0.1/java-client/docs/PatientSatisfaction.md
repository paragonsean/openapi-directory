

# PatientSatisfaction

Veteran-reported satisfaction score for health care services at VA health facilities.

## Properties

| Name | Type | Description | Notes |
|------------ | ------------- | ------------- | -------------|
|**primaryCareRoutine** | **Float** | Percentage of Veterans who say they usually or always get an appointment when they need routine attention at a primary care location. NOTE: Veterans are rating their satisfaction of getting an appointment for a routine primary care visit. |  [optional] |
|**primaryCareUrgent** | **Float** | Percentage of Veterans who say they usually or always get an appointment when they need urgent attention at a primary care location. NOTE: Veterans are rating their satisfaction of getting an appointment for an urgent primary care visit, NOT an urgent care visit. |  [optional] |
|**specialtyCareRoutine** | **Float** | Percentage of Veterans who say they usually or always get an appointment when they need routine attention at a specialty care location. NOTE: Veterans are rating their satisfaction of getting an appointment for a routine specialty care visit. |  [optional] |
|**specialtyCareUrgent** | **Float** | Percentage of Veterans who say they usually or always get an appointment when they need urgent attention at a specialty care location. NOTE: Veterans are rating their satisfaction of getting an appointment for an urgent specialty care visit, NOT an urgent care visit. |  [optional] |
